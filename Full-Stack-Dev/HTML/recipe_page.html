Biscuits and Gravy Pasta

This was a thrown-together meal the night before grocery day. This recipe combines breakfast sausage links, traditional rue-style gravy, and penne pasta. The dish proved to be savory and surprisingly reminiscent of biscuits and gravy.

Ingredients

  • Breakfast Sausage Links
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Penne Pasta
  • Seasoned Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Remove sausage from casings.
  2. Boil penne to al dente and strain.
  3. Brown sausage.
  4. Strain grease from cooked sausage, saving grease.
  5. Set cooked sausage aside.
  6. Melt butter in saucepan.
  7. Mix in flour and spices.
  8. Mix in water, grease, and milk whisking constantly.
  9. Mix in cooked sausage.
  10. Mix pasta and sauce, sautee to desired temp and serve.
